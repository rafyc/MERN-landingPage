Sign Up

Sign up to the app by filling the fields with you password and e-mail. If you're not already register, you can create a new account by choosing a password, a pseudo and your e-mail.

Create Profile

Create and update your profile by choosing a picture that represent you. You can also tell us more about youself in the bio description. Here you are able to see the students you are following and those who followed you.

Enjoy the app

Start interact with other students by posting images, text and videos. Dont worry if you made a mistake, you could always edit or delete your post. Show them you have enjoyed their post by liking them.
